Developers Diary – Ploobs Tech Demo Game


As a lot of people know we´ve been developing the Ploobs Engine for 2 years, and in this time we’ve reached a point we never dreamed of, talking about level of details and performance of our engine, but every time someone ask us to show our features we found ourselves in a big trouble. So we decided to go beyond and venture in the gamedesign world. We know there will be a long road, but we decided to share our experience with you, our audience.

In the next post we will tell you our evolution essays, like a post mortem before morten, like a “ante mortem”.

The post will be probably short, but full of our experience.

We will talk about:

  • Concept art
  • Gamedesign
  • Programming
  • Storytelling
We will try to show the minor details of our decisions, maybe you will think about how many times we were idiots, but there is all right, to learn means we´ve made mistakes before.
I hope you enjoy as much as we will.
